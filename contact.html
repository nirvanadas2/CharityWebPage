Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access your mental health services?

Our services can be accessed by calling our main line at 1-800-MINDWELL or through our online intake form. For crisis support, call our 24/7 crisis line at 1-800-273-TALK. Many of our services are available remotely via telehealth platforms.

Do you offer services in languages other than English?

Yes, we provide services in Spanish, Mandarin, and several other languages through our multilingual staff and interpreter services. Please let us know your language preference when you contact us.

What are your fees for services?

We operate on a sliding scale based on income and offer many free services thanks to donor support. No one is turned away due to inability to pay. Our intake team can discuss payment options during your initial consultation.

How can I volunteer with MindWell?

We welcome volunteers in many capacities! Visit our Get Involved page to see current opportunities and fill out a volunteer application. All volunteers complete training appropriate to their role.

How do I make a donation to support your work?

You can donate online through our secure donation portal, by calling our office, or by mailing a check to our address. We also accept stock donations and can provide information about planned giving options.

Are my conversations with your staff confidential?

Absolutely. We adhere to strict confidentiality standards in compliance with HIPAA and other privacy laws. Your information will never be shared without your written consent, except in rare circumstances where there is immediate danger to yourself or others.
