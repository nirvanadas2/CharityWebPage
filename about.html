Our Story

Founded in 2010, MindWell Foundation began as a small community initiative to address the growing mental health crisis in our city. What started as a peer support group meeting in a local church basement has grown into a nationally recognized organization serving thousands each year.

Our founder, Dr. Sarah Chen, a clinical psychologist, recognized the gap between those needing mental health support and those able to access it. She envisioned an organization that would remove barriers to care through innovative programs, community partnerships, and a commitment to making mental health support accessible to all.

Today, we operate in 12 cities across the country with over 200 staff members and 1,500 trained volunteers. We've helped more than 50,000 individuals find the support they need to improve their mental well-being.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities to achieve mental wellness through accessible support, education, and advocacy. We believe everyone deserves compassionate care and the tools to thrive emotionally and psychologically.

Our Vision

A world where mental health is valued as highly as physical health, where stigma is eliminated, and where all people have access to the support they need to live fulfilling lives.
